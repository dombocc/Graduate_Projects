This project was the VAST Challenge 2018 MC2 question. 

This project utilized html and javascript, including d3. Tableau was also used to explore the data and visualize trends that couldn't be seen in the raw data.

Each month has approximately 5 seconds of screen time to show the datapoints of each loction. The size of the circle and the color of the cirlce and print signify the saturation of chemical in the water given by probes at each location. 

This visualization was designed to help answer the questions as to whether a nearby company was in fact dumping chemcials and pollution the waterways.
